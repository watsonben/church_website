A little about us

We're a small, community-based church in Whitby, New Zealand and we believe that church isn't just about how loudly you can sing on a Sunday. We try to maintain a balance between the worship, teaching, and youth group, and we do alright. Overall we focus on the family and community aspect of being Christians, acting what Jesus said when he told us to show him to the world and those around us.

What we stand for

Loving God
Loving people
Learning it
Living it

We believe that above everything else, God is the priority. Jesus said we are to love Him with everything we have and that's our main goal!

Secondly, we focus on people. The community. Jesus called us to love people like we love ourselves, so we try to act on that and get it right as much as we can.

Our third focus is the bible. Learning how God wants us to live, and the things he has in store for us. We also find it's a great way to keep in touch with Him and figure out his will for our lives.

Finally, knowing how God wants us to live, we place high value on actually living it. Not being Sunday Christians, but going out into the real world and showing real Jesus to real people.
